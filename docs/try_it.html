Grab the Makeskill Templates

Check out the UX Guidelines. These templates should simulate how your actual cards will look on the two devices. The templates reflect what the Echo platform supports today. When designing the Echo cards, remember to design for voice first, and then augment it with cards.

Deploy and Test Prototypes

After building the prototype through Makeskill CLI, then you can preview your cards through the Amazon Developer simulator or on your actual devices!
